cr.vision.edits.placement module¶

Functions for placing one image inside another

cr.vision.edits.placement.place_image_at(target_image, src_image, roi_mask, x_pos, y_pos)[source]¶

Patches src_image into the target_image at the location specified by x_pos and y_pos on the pixels identified by the roi_mask

Note

This function overwrites the roi in target image.
